About Us

Café Her og Der is a family business founded in the summer of 2016, starting with our small rolling coffee bar that you can always find near the Deer Park.

Our wish was to create a cozy space where our customers can get the full hygge experience. With that in mind, two years ago we opened another location, a small basement coffee shop where you can sit down, relax and let the time slow down while you listen to some good music. We care about our customers and we strive to bring them the best products possible and that’s why our coffee is made from organic, freshly baked beans. Our baked goods are also made from scratch out of high-quality ingredients in the stove oven.
